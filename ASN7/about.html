About

While newer technology can be innovative and flashy, we all sometimes love using our old hardware just for being charming.
In TechnoArmor, we strive to help everyone breath new life in retro devices and enchance everyone's experience with them.
We focus on quality on our products and some of the most helpful guides to ensure that everything goes well.
We strive to be everyone's go to place for anything retro modding related.
